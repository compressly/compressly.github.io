Windows Defender / SmartScreen

Some users may see a SmartScreen or Windows Defender warning for new releases of small apps distributed outside major stores. This is a false positive in many cases and can happen for low-distribution apps or unsigned binaries.

  • If you see a "Windows protected your PC" message, click "More info" and then "Run anyway".
  • If Defender blocks the download, you can temporarily disable real-time protection or add an exclusion for the downloaded file/folder.
